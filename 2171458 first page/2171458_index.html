Transmedia

In this project, I created a transmedia design that allowed to introduce users that don't like videogames to a comic that allowed them to experience part of the main story of the project without the burning of not knowing how to play the videogame. Some people don't like videogames, but they like comics. Some people don't like comics, but they are interested in podcasts or other type of entertainment. With a proper transmedia diffusion, your project can reach users outside your common niche.
